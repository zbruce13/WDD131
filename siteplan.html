Local Hiking and Outdoor Adventure Club - Site Plan

Site Name

Local Hiking and Outdoor Adventure Club

This name identifies the website as a resource for local hikers and outdoor enthusiasts, reflecting the mission of promoting safe, enjoyable outdoor activities in the community.

Site Purpose

The website provides a hub for local hiking and outdoor activities. Visitors can find:

  • Information about nearby trails
  • Upcoming group hikes and events
  • Safety tips and gear recommendations
  • Photos and testimonials from club members
  • Contact information and membership registration

Scenarios

  • Where can I find safe and scenic trails suitable for beginners?
  • How do I register for upcoming group hikes?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #2E8B57 – used for headings, links, and accents.

Secondary Color: #F0E68C – used for background highlights and sections.

Typography

  • Arial, sans-serif – main body text
  • Georgia, serif – headings (h1, h2, h3)
  • Additional fonts may be used for special callout sections if needed
